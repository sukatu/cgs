Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the CGS Network?

Visit our Network page to create an account. Membership is open to directors, lawyers, bankers, regulators, scholars, and governance professionals interested in advancing governance in Africa.

Are CGS events open to the public?

Many of our webinars are open for public registration, while flagship series sessions may have limited capacity. Network members receive priority registration. Check our Events page for details.

How can I request training for my organization?

Use the contact form above or visit our Training page to request a proposal. We offer customized programs for boards, teams, and organizations.

Can I contribute to the CGS Blog?

We welcome contributions from governance professionals. Please contact us using the form above with your article idea and brief author bio. Network members are especially encouraged to contribute.
